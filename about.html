About The Shop

We are a small bakery that specializes in cinnamon rolls, from our famous classic cinnamon roll to a variety of artisan flavors. Think cinnamon bun, but not just cinnamon. We've been baking buns since 1992 and we're proud to always use local ingredients from Pittsburgh!
