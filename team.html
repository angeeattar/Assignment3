The Team

Bun Bun is a truly unique bakeshop with the widest variety of buns! Think cinnamon bun, but not just cinnamon. We've been baking buns since 1982 and we're proud to always use local ingredients!

Jeanie

Jeanie is a professional chef and food science nerd. She hails from Westmoreland County and began her illustrious foray into the culinary world at age 15 as most chefs do – washing dishes. Before starting Bun Bun's, Jeanie worked as a chef at some of Pittsburgh’s finest restaurants including Eleven and Salt of the Earth, as well as the two-Michelin starred Yoann Conte restaurant in Annecy, France.

Lauren

Lauren has a profound sweet tooth and a long love affair with buns. In fact, she signed up for youth sports in Pittsburgh’s South Hills mostly just to get ice cream after the games. At Millie’s, Lauren gets to exercise both sides of her brain, handling everything from finances to branding and the entire customer experience.
